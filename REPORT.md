# Amazon Sales Analytics Report

## Executive Summary

This project analyzes Amazon marketplace product data to uncover insights related to pricing strategy, customer engagement, product quality, and demand concentration.

The analysis found that Electronics and Accessories dominate customer attention, while discounts alone have limited influence on customer ratings. Product quality signals such as ratings and review volume appear more valuable than aggressive markdown strategies.

---

## Business Problem

In competitive e-commerce environments, sellers must understand:

- Which categories attract the most demand
- How discounts impact perception
- Where premium pricing is sustainable
- Which segments deserve inventory focus

---

## Dataset Overview

Source: Kaggle Amazon Sales Dataset

Features used:

- Product Name
- Category
- Discounted Price
- Actual Price
- Discount Percentage
- Rating
- Rating Count

---

## Data Preparation

Completed steps:

- Converted prices into numeric values
- Cleaned discount percentages
- Converted rating counts to numeric
- Split category hierarchy into main and sub categories
- Checked null values and datatypes

---

## Key Findings

### Pricing

- Electronics categories showed high discount activity.
- Premium electronics maintained strong pricing levels.

### Ratings

- Discounts had weak negative correlation with ratings.
- Quality appeared more important than promotions.

### Demand

- Electronics generated highest review volume.
- Accessories and mobile-related products dominated subcategories.

---

## Recommendations

1. Focus inventory on high-demand accessory segments.
2. Improve quality before increasing discounts.
3. Protect pricing in premium trusted categories.

---

## Conclusion

Marketplace demand is concentrated in technology-driven categories, while customer satisfaction depends more on quality than discount depth.
