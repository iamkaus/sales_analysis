# Amazon Sales Data Analysis

## Introduction

This document summarizes analytical findings from Amazon product sales data.

The goal was to transform raw product listing data into business intelligence insights around pricing, quality, and customer demand.

---

## Data Preparation

Completed preprocessing steps:

- Removed ₹ symbols and commas
- Converted prices to numeric values
- Converted review counts to numeric
- Extracted category levels:
  - main_category
  - sub_category

---

## Pricing Analysis

### Average Discounts by Category

Competitive categories relied more heavily on markdowns.

### Premium Pricing Segments

Electronics and appliance categories sustained higher average prices.

### Discount vs Rating

Weak negative correlation observed between discounts and ratings.

Interpretation:

Large discounts do not guarantee stronger customer satisfaction.

---

## Product Quality Analysis

### Highest Rated Trusted Products

Products with 1000+ reviews and strong ratings showed reliable customer trust.

### Most Reviewed Products

High review counts were concentrated in Electronics and Accessories.

---

## Demand Analysis

### Main Categories

Highest customer engagement:

1. Electronics
2. Computers & Accessories
3. Home & Kitchen

### Top Subcategories

- Headphones / Earbuds
- Mobiles & Accessories
- Accessories & Peripherals
- Kitchen Appliances

---

## Final Insight

Customer demand concentrates in technology-led segments, while ratings depend more on quality than pricing promotions.
