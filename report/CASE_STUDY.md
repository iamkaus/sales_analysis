# Case Study: Amazon Marketplace Pricing & Demand Intelligence

## Background

E-commerce platforms rely on pricing strategy, product reputation, and category demand to remain competitive.

Understanding how these variables interact can help sellers improve conversions and profitability.

---

## Challenge

Determine:

- Which categories are most price competitive?
- Which categories command premium pricing?
- What drives customer ratings?
- Where is customer demand concentrated?

---

## Approach

Using Amazon product data, the following analyses were performed:

- Category segmentation
- Price and discount analysis
- Customer rating analysis
- Review volume demand analysis
- Correlation testing

---

## Results

### Pricing

Electronics remained premium-priced despite discounting.

### Ratings

Discounts showed weak negative relationship with ratings.

### Demand

Technology and accessories dominated engagement.

### Opportunity Areas

- Audio devices
- Mobile accessories
- Kitchen appliances

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Grow catalog depth in high-demand subcategories
2. Use discounts for tactical growth only
3. Focus on product quality to improve ratings
4. Preserve premium pricing in trusted categories

---

## Key Lesson

Demand can be purchased with discounts, but trust must be earned through quality.
